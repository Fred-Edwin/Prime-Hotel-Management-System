# Prime Hotel — manual demo recording script

For recording yourself walking through the app with narration (screen recording + your voice). Covers the same 4 flows as the automated videos. Log in with the name shown and PIN (see your private credentials note — not repeated here) for each part.

Speak naturally — the lines below are a guide, not a script to read word-for-word.

---

## Video 1 — Admin (WaPrecious)

**Login:** WaPrecious / (use the current admin PIN)

1. On the login screen, say: *"Staff log in with just their name and a PIN — no emails or passwords to remember."* Tap your name, enter the PIN.
2. You'll land on the **Dashboard**. Say: *"This is what the owner sees every day — total sales, cost of goods, profit, all calculated automatically from what staff log."*
3. Tap between **Today / Week / Month** at the top. Say: *"She can flip between today's numbers, the week, or the month without doing any manual tallying."*
4. Scroll down to show the profit breakdown and wastage figure. Say: *"Wastage is tracked separately from cost, so she can see exactly how much spoilage is costing her — not buried inside expenses."*
5. Tap into the **Ledger** (from the dashboard). Say: *"This is the full transaction history — every stock entry, order, and expense, in one place."*
6. Go to **Items**. Say: *"This is the master catalog — prices, categories. When she updates a price here, it doesn't change what already happened in the past — old entries keep the price that was true at the time."*
7. Briefly show **Ingredients**, **Delivery Locations**, and **Staff** screens. Say for delivery locations: *"Delivery zones and their fees are set up once here — staff just pick a zone, they never type in a fee themselves, so there's no room for a wrong number."*
8. Log out.

## Video 2 — Restaurant staff (Sarah Makena)

**Login:** Sarah Makena / (use the current staff PIN)

1. Land on **Today's Entry**. Say: *"This is what a cashier sees at the end of the day — everything they need to log, mobile-first."*
2. Tap a category chip (e.g. Snacks) to filter. Say: *"They can filter by category or just search."*
3. Type in the search bar (e.g. "Tea"). Say: *"Or search directly for an item."* Clear the search.
4. Tap the **+** stepper on 2–3 items to log quantities sold. Say: *"Each tap logs a unit sold — no typing, no math. The 'Available' figure updates live so they can't accidentally oversell more than what's in stock."*
5. Tap **Log wastage** on one item, add a wastage count. Say: *"If something spoils or breaks, it gets logged here separately — this is what feeds that wastage figure the owner sees on the dashboard."*
6. Point out the running total bar at the bottom. Say: *"They can see the running total before they even save."*
7. Tap **Save**. Wait for the success confirmation. Say: *"And that's it — saved instantly."*
8. Navigate to **Expenses** via the bottom nav. Fill in a category (e.g. Electricity), an amount, and a short note. Tap **Add expense**. Say: *"Day-to-day expenses — like a KPLC top-up or charcoal — get logged the same way, right from the phone."*
9. Log out.

## Video 4 — Orders and Store Manager

### Part A — Orders (Sarah Makena)

**Login:** Sarah Makena / (use the current staff PIN)

1. Go to **Orders** from the bottom nav. Say: *"This replaces the old WhatsApp order process — deliveries and pickups both get logged here."*
2. Type a customer name. Tap **Delivery**. Select a delivery zone from the dropdown. Point at the **Fee** field. Say: *"The fee fills in automatically the moment they pick a zone — staff never type a delivery fee themselves, so it can't be entered wrong."*
3. Search for 1–2 items, tap the steppers to add them to the order.
4. Show the order summary (items + delivery fee = total).
5. Tap **Save order**. Say: *"Saved — and this feeds the same stock and sales figures as a till sale, so nothing gets double-counted or missed."*
6. Fill a second order: a customer name, tap **Pickup** this time (note the delivery zone/fee fields disappear), add an item, save. Say: *"Pickup orders are even simpler — no zone needed."*

### Part B — Store manager (Janiffer Maina)

**Login:** Janiffer Maina / (use the current staff PIN)

1. Go to **Store** from the bottom nav (only store managers see this tab). Say: *"This is separate from the menu items — it's for tracking raw ingredients like rice, cooking oil, tomatoes, the things that go into cooking rather than what's sold directly."*
2. Search for an ingredient (e.g. Rice). Tap the **Received** stepper to log new stock delivered. Tap the **Used in cooking** stepper to log what was consumed.
3. Show the running total at the bottom.
4. Tap **Save**. Say: *"And that's the full loop — from ingredients coming in, to menu items being sold, to the owner seeing real profit at the end of the day, all without a single Excel sheet."*
5. Log out.
